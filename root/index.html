About Me

I am a Computer Science student at the American University of Beirut with a strong interest in solving complex problems through technology, data, and strategic thinking. My work sits at the intersection of software engineering, data analysis, and consulting, where I enjoy building systems, uncovering insights, and designing solutions that create real-world impact.
